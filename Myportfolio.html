Cricket History

Cricket has always been a sport full of rich history and unforgettable moments. From the first test match in 1877 to the modern-day T20 leagues, the evolution of cricket has been extraordinary.

  • Cricket was first played in the 16th century in England.
  • The first-ever Cricket World Cup was held in 1975.
  • ODI cricket revolutionized the game, bringing a fast-paced, exciting format.
